J. Terrell Allen

UX Designer

Education has no boundary. As a User Experence Designer I seek inspiration from my history as a Pastry Chef, Corporate Trainer, Photographer, and Author. In the classroom or the kitchen, through a camera or a computer, I seek the aestheticism and practicality in everything I do. Ingrained with creativity, I have the ability to learn quickly and adapt to a variety of environments.

Chicago enticed me with its diversity and culture; it ensnared me with its industry and innovation. I was born in Alabama, and my home pervades my soul. From the wild hills in the north to the tranquil Gulf coast in the south, that inexpressible ‘Southern-something’ stays with me. Its rural, agricultural heritage is embedded in me.

I believe in simplicity and brevity.
